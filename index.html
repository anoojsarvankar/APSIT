ENGINEERING PRACTICES AND FUTURISTIC TECHNOLOGIES

JEPFT is a scholarly interdisciplinary journal focusing on theories, methods and applications in Engineering and Technology. It covers all areas of Engineering and Technology & publishing original research articles. The aim of the journal is to provide fast publication of referred high quality original research papers in all areas of engineering and technology.
